Genealogy requests and house or business history requests are welcome. Not only do we learn more about the Town of Stafford and its past residents, but it also allows us to connect with people outside of our group with an interest in Stafford's history.

There is no charge for these services as we are an entirely non-profit, volunteer-run organization but small donations are absolutely welcomed. Checks can be made out to "Stafford Historical Society".
